Odin Recipe: Chicken and Rice Bowl

Description

This is a simple recipe to create a staple, a Pasta with Chicken. While being very basic it has immense nutritional value and is a blank canvas where you can add endless possibilites to match your taste.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Cook Chicken
  2. Boil Pasta
  3. Add sauce and toppings to taste
  4. Enjoy
